Boat Tour Offers Glimpse into Life on British Waterways

Held in London’s Little Venice, the Canalway Cavalcade offers a vibrant look at life on Britain’s waterways. Green Bean Media UK • 24th April 2025

Birmingham Bin Strike Raises Public Health Concerns

Birmingham’s refuse workers have been on indefinite strike for over a month, protesting pay cuts and the removal of Waste Recycling and Collection Officer (WRCO) roles. The City Council declared a major incident as rubbish piled up across the city. Over Easter, the council deployed remaining staff and claimed to have cleared 85% of waste. However, residents in deprived areas say their bins are still overflowing, raising concerns over unequal cleanup. A pest controller has warned of growing health risks, including rising vermin activity and disease threats, if the strike continues.

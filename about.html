About Me

Hello, 你好。

Cathy Chen, also known as Chen Chih Ching, is an award-winning multimedia journalist - from Hong Kong, was born in Taiwan, and based in London.

Driven by a passion for lifelong learning, Cathy earned her Master's degree in Digital Media from University College London (UCL), and work as a NCTJ trainee.

With a diverse cultural background, Cathy is fervent about covering a broad spectrum of news topics. She is particularly dedicated to shedding light on social issues and human rights concerns, advocating for justice by amplifying the voices of underrepresented groups.

Cathy，是一位得獎的多媒體記者——來自香港，出生於台灣，現居於倫敦。對於學習的熱衷，Cathy來到英國，完成倫敦大學學院（UCL）的數位媒體碩士學位，並擔任NCTJ（英國國家記者培訓理事會）的實習生。擁有多元文化背景，Cathy致力於報導各種新聞話題。她注重社會議題和人權問題，希望透過新聞的力量，為大眾發聲，實現民主和正義。
